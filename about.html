The Story of Lemme

We are a collective of artists, engineers, and storytellers building delightful casual games that connect millions every day.

Our Mission

To craft joyful escapes that reward curiosity and kindness. Whether you have two minutes or an entire evening, Lemme invites you into a world that feels instantly welcoming.

Studio Values

  • Playfulness inspires every decision we make.
  • Accessibility is a non-negotiable design pillar.
  • Community feedback guides each update.

Milestones

2018

Lemme Founded

A four-person team in Seattle set out to make casual games that felt premium.

2019

First Platform Launch

Turbo Drift debuted with 1 million players in the first 90 days.

2021

Global Expansion

Opened live-ops hubs in Berlin and Singapore to support nonstop updates.

2023

24-Game Portfolio

Reached our 24th shipped title with the launch of Prism Parade.
